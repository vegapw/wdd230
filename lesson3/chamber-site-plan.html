Overview

Purpose Site

The purpose of the site is to promote the business interest of the members of Sabaneta and promote the area, attracting aditional business, tourism and people.

Audience

There will be several target audiences for this website. We will mainly target all people who would like to learn more about currently running business in Sabaneta or any other interest regarding events and opportunities to create business in here.
